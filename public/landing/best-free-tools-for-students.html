💡 Study Smarter

3 Student Success Tips Using Free Tools

Practical strategies real students use to study more effectively — with InfinityKit tools woven in naturally.

01

Use Active Listening to Retain More

Re-reading notes is one of the least effective study methods. Instead, paste your lecture notes into the Text to Speech tool and listen while doing something with your hands — drawing diagrams, making flashcards, or going for a walk. This dual-channel encoding dramatically improves long-term memory retention according to learning science research.

🔊 Try Text to Speech →

Plan Your Week Before the Week Plans You

02

Every Sunday, open the Todo List and add every assignment, reading, and exam coming up in the next two weeks. Break big tasks into subtasks — "write essay" becomes "outline," "draft intro," "draft body," "proofread." The simple act of externalising your workload removes the mental overhead that causes procrastination and panic.

✅ Try Todo List →
03

Know Your Grade Math Before Finals

Don't wait until the night before your final to figure out what score you need. Use the Exam Calculator early — it tells you exactly what you need to hit your target grade based on your current scores and weighting. Then you can allocate study time strategically instead of panicking across all your subjects at once.
