The Importance of a Fresh Start

This tool allows you to permanently clear all your stored expense data from the tracker, giving you a completely fresh start whenever you need it. In personal finance, tracking every penny is crucial for understanding your spending habits. However, there are times when your historical data no longer serves you.

Perhaps you have completed a specific saving goal, transitioned to a new budget year, or simply want to erase past financial mistakes from your dashboard to reduce anxiety. Purging old data allows you to focus on the present and future with clarity.

🛠️

How to Use the Reset Tool

  1. Log In: To clear your data across all connected devices simultaneously, please log in.
  2. Click the Reset button to initiate the clearing process.
  3. Read the warning prompt carefully. This action cannot be undone.
  4. Confirm your action to finalize the reset.
  5. Your dashboard will return to zero, ready for new entries.

When Should You Reset Your Data?

Consider using this tool in the following scenarios:

📅 New Fiscal Year

Clear out last year's expenses to start tracking the new year with a clean slate and zeroed-out categories.

🎯 Goal Completion

If you were tracking expenses for a specific vacation or project, clear the data once the project is over.

🧹 Privacy Protection

If you are selling or giving away your device, use this tool to ensure your financial history is wiped clean.

🚀

Why a Login is Required for Global Clear

If you use our app on multiple devices without an account, your data is stored separately in each browser's local storage. Clicking reset on your laptop will not affect your phone. We require a login for global clearing to:

  • Identify All Connected Devices: Send a secure signal to all browsers where you are logged in to delete their local database.
  • Verify Authority: Ensure that someone else using your device cannot accidentally wipe your cloud-saved budget.
