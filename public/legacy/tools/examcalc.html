Navigating Your Academic Journey

This tool simplifies the process of calculating your total exam marks and overall percentage, helping you track your academic progress with ease. For students, understanding where you stand academically is key to setting goals and reducing exam stress. However, adding up marks from multiple subjects and calculating weighted percentages can be tedious.

Our Exam Marks Calculator does the heavy lifting for you. Simply enter your scores, and it provides a clear breakdown of your total marks and final percentage instantly.

🛠️

How to Use the Exam Marks Calculator

  1. Log In: To save your grades and track your progress across semesters, please log in or create a free account.
  2. Enter the marks you obtained for each of your subjects in the provided fields.
  3. Enter the maximum possible marks for each subject if they differ from the default.
  4. Click the Calculate button to process the numbers.
  5. Instantly view your total marks and your final percentage.
✨

Key Features & Benefits

  • Progress Tracking: Log in to save your results and see your improvement over time on a graph.
  • Weighted Averages: Support for subjects that carry more weight toward your final GPA.
  • Fast Calculation: Get your results immediately after inputting your marks.
  • Simple Input: Clear and easy-to-use fields for multiple subjects.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is the calculation accurate? ▼
Yes, the tool uses standard mathematical formulas to ensure every percentage is calculated with 100% accuracy based on your inputs.
Q: Can I enter any number of subjects? ▼
Yes, you can add and calculate marks for as many subjects as you need. The tool is dynamic.
Q: Is login required to use the tool? ▼
Yes, a free account is required to save your grades and track your academic progress over time. One-off calculations do not require login.
Q: Does it support GPA conversion? ▼
Not in the basic version. We plan to add percentage-to-GPA conversion for logged-in users in the future.
