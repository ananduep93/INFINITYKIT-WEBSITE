The Science of Sound and Concentration

This tool allows you to create custom ambient soundscapes to help you focus, relax, or sleep. In an open-plan office or a noisy home, silence is rare. However, complete silence isn't actually the best environment for focus for many people.

Studies show that moderate ambient noise (like the hum of a coffee shop or the sound of falling rain) can improve cognitive performance by masking sudden, distracting sounds. This tool puts you in control of your auditory environment.

🛠️

Why a Login is Required for Binaural Beats

Playing standard audio files in your browser is simple and free. However, generating precise Binaural Beats requires synthesizing two slightly different frequencies in real-time using the Web Audio API. We require a login for these features to:

  • Save Custom Presets: If you find the exact volume mix that helps you focus perfectly, you can save it to your account for one-click access tomorrow.
  • Limit API Abuse: Continuous synthesis requires CPU power. Restricting it to registered users ensures high performance for everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Do I need headphones? ▼
For standard ambient sounds, speakers are fine. However, **Binaural Beats require headphones** to deliver different frequencies to each ear.
Q: Is login required to use the tool? ▼
Yes, a free account is required to save your custom ambient mixes for future sessions or to unlock premium soundscapes like Binaural beats. Basic ambient sounds are free.
Q: Will it drain my phone battery? ▼
The tool uses optimized loops to minimize CPU usage, but playing continuous audio will always use more battery than a static page.
