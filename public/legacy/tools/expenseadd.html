The Foundation of Financial Freedom

This tool helps you quickly and easily log your daily expenditures, allowing you to track exactly where your money is going and manage your finances better. Financial freedom starts with awareness. You cannot manage what you do not measure, and tracking daily expenses is the single most effective habit for improving your financial health.

Our Add Expense tool makes it frictionless to log your spending on the go. Whether it is a morning coffee, a utility bill, or a major purchase, entering it takes just a few seconds.

🛠️

How to Use the Add Expense Tool

  1. Log In: To save your expenses across devices and access advanced reporting, please log in or create a free account.
  2. Enter the total amount of your expense in the amount field.
  3. Select the appropriate category (e.g., Food, Travel, Entertainment).
  4. Optionally, add a short note or description for more details.
  5. Click the Add button to save your entry.
