The Joy of Random Decision Making

This tool helps you make random choices in a fun and interactive way using a virtual spinning wheel. Decision fatigue is a real psychological phenomenon. After making hundreds of minor choices throughout the day, our brains get tired, leading to poor choices or procrastination.

A spin wheel removes the stress of choice by introducing an element of chance. Whether you are deciding where to go for lunch with coworkers or picking a random winner for a stream giveaway, the spinning wheel makes the process transparent, fair, and entertaining.

🛠️

How to Use the Spin Wheel

  1. Log In: To save your custom wheels for future use or to apply custom branding, please log in.
  2. Enter all the options you want to choose from in the provided fields.
  3. Customize the colors of the slices if desired.
  4. Click the Spin button to start the wheel turning.
  5. Wait for the wheel to slow down and stop on your random result.
✨

Key Features & Benefits

  • Smooth Physics: Realistic deceleration makes every spin suspenseful.
  • Saved Wheels: Logged-in users can save wheels like "Dinner Options" or "Team Members".
  • Sound Effects: Satisfying clicking sounds as the ticker passes pins.
  • Full Screen Mode: Perfect for projecting in a classroom or during a live stream.

Why a Login is Required for Custom Wheels

Setting up a wheel with 20 custom options and specific colors takes time. If you clear your browser cookies, that wheel is lost. We offer a saving feature to preserve your hard work. We require a login for this feature to:

  • Store Configurations in the Cloud: Access your specific wheels on any device instantly.
  • Upload Custom Logos: Add your brand logo to the center of the wheel for professional presentations.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is it truly random? ▼
Yes, the tool uses a cryptographically secure random number generator to determine the stopping angle, ensuring fairness.
Q: Is login required to use the tool? ▼
Yes, a free account is required to save your custom wheels for future use or to create designs with custom branding. Basic spinning is free.
Q: Can I remove options after they win? ▼
Yes, there is an option to auto-remove the winning slice so it cannot be picked twice in a row.
