The Science of Structured Decisions

This tool helps you evaluate and compare two different options side-by-side to help you decide which one is better for your needs. Making big decisions (like which job offer to accept or which laptop to buy) is mentally exhausting. Our brains struggle to hold multiple variables at once.

A decision matrix or comparison grid forces you to write down the features, pros, and cons of each option. By assigning scores to each factor, you turn an emotional, gut-feeling choice into a logical, data-driven decision. This tool provides that structure.

🛠️

How to Use the Choice Comparator

  1. Log In: To save your comparisons for future reference or to generate a shareable link to send to colleagues, please log in.
  2. Enter the name of your first choice in the "Option A" header.
  3. Enter the name of your second choice in the "Option B" header.
  4. Add rows for each criteria you want to evaluate (e.g., Price, Speed, Design).
  5. Score each option from 1-10 or write pros and cons in the fields.
✨

Key Features & Benefits

  • Side-by-Side Grid: Clear layout that prevents bias by showing both options equally.
  • Weighted Criteria: Logged-in users can state that "Price" is twice as important as "Color."
  • Auto-Summing Scores: The tool automatically calculates which option won based on your ratings.
  • Shareable Links: Generate a unique URL to let your partner or boss vote on the options.
💡

When Do You Need This Tool?

Structured comparison is useful in both personal and professional life:

💼 Job Offer Evaluation

Compare Salary, Commute Time, Growth Potential, and Company Culture between two competing offers.

🏠 Apartment Hunting

Evaluate Option A (closer to work but smaller) against Option B (larger but more expensive) logically.

💻 Tech Purchases

Compare specifications, battery life, and prices of two different smartphone models before buying.

🚀

Why a Login is Required for Saving

Creating a temporary grid in your browser is free. However, if you are working on a complex decision that takes days of research, you need to save your progress. We require a login for these features to:

  • Prevent Data Loss: If you clear your browser cache, your hard-earned comparison data will be lost unless saved to an account.
  • Enable Collaboration: Share the link with your family or team so they can add their own scores to the grid.
❓

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Can I compare 3 or more options? ▼
The current interface is optimized for A/B comparison. Logged-in users can unlock a multi-column view for up to 5 options.
Q: Is login required to use the tool? ▼
Yes, a free account is required to save your comparisons for future reference or to generate a shareable link to send to colleagues. Basic 2-way comparison is free.
Q: Does it use AI to recommend the winner? ▼
No, the tool relies entirely on the scores and weights you provide to calculate the mathematical winner.
