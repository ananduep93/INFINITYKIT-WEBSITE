The Importance of Strong Passwords

This tool helps you create strong, unique, and secure passwords instantly to protect your online accounts from unauthorized access. In an era where data breaches are common, using the same password across multiple sites is one of the biggest security risks you can take.

A strong password acts as the first line of defense against cybercriminals. Cyber attackers use automated scripts to guess passwords (brute-forcing) or try passwords leaked from other breaches (credential stuffing). To resist these attacks, a password must be long, complex, and entirely random.

Best Practices for Password Security

Generating a strong password is only half the battle. Follow these rules to maximize your safety:

🚫 Never Reuse Passwords

Every account must have a unique password. If one site leaks your data, your other accounts remain safe.

📦 Use a Password Manager

Don't try to memorize complex passwords. Use a secure manager (or our vault) to remember them for you.

🛡️ Enable 2FA

Even with a strong password, always enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for an extra layer of defense.

Why a Login is Required for the Vault

Generating passwords locally is free and requires no account. However, storing those passwords securely so you can access them later requires a dedicated account. We require a login for the vault to:

  • Implement End-to-End Encryption: Your vault is encrypted with a master key derived from your login credentials. We cannot see your passwords.

Q: Is it safe to generate passwords online? ▼
Yes, our tool generates the passwords locally in your browser using JavaScript. No data is sent over the internet unless you choose to save it to your logged-in vault.

Q: What makes a password strong? ▼
