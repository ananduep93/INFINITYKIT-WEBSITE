The Power of Feedback in Decision Making

This dashboard allows you to manage all the surveys you have created using the Infinity Kit Survey Hub. Gathering feedback is the most effective way to understand your audience, validate product ideas, or organize events.

Instead of guessing what your customers or users want, you can ask them directly. A well-designed survey provides structured data that can be analyzed to reveal trends and actionable insights. This page acts as your control center for those insights.

🛠️

How to Manage Your Surveys

  1. Log In: To access this dashboard and view your created surveys, please log in.
  2. Browse your list of active and archived surveys.
  3. Click View Responses to see the data collected so far.
  4. Click Share to copy the public link to your clipboard.
  5. Use the Delete button to remove surveys you no longer need.
✨

Key Features & Benefits

  • Real-Time Stats: See response counts and completion rates at a glance.
  • CSV Export: Download your response data to analyze in Excel or Google Sheets.
  • Link Management: Easily disable surveys when you have enough data.
  • Mobile Optimized: Manage your surveys on the go from your phone or tablet.

Why a Login is Required for Survey Management

Collecting survey responses requires a centralized database to store the answers submitted by the public. We require a login for the survey creator to:

🔒 Protect Your Data

Only you should be able to see the private answers submitted to your survey. Login ensures data privacy.

📊 Enable Analytics

We need to associate surveys with your specific user ID to generate the charts and graphs in your dashboard.

📧 Notifications

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I create a survey? ▼
Go to the Survey Hub and click 'Create Survey'. You can add questions, choices, and set a title.
Q: Is login required to use the tool? ▼
Yes, a free account is required to save your surveys, generate shareable links, and view response analytics.
Q: How many responses can I collect? ▼
Free accounts can collect up to 100 responses per survey. Premium accounts have no limits.
