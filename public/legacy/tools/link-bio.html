Maximizing Your Social Media Real Estate

Social media platforms like Instagram and TikTok only allow a single clickable link in your profile bio. This is a massive limitation for creators, businesses, and influencers who want to direct followers to their website, YouTube channel, online store, and latest blog post simultaneously.

Use this tool to create a custom, mobile-optimized landing page that hosts all your important links in one beautiful place. Instead of constantly changing your bio link, you just update your Infinity Kit hub.

🛠️

How to Use the Link-in-Bio Builder

  1. Log In: To create a permanent shareable link or to view click analytics for your profile, please log in.
  2. Enter your name, profile picture, and a short bio.
  3. Add your links by typing the title and pasting the URL.
  4. Choose a theme or customize the colors to match your brand.
  5. Generate your short link and paste it into your Instagram profile.
✨

Key Features & Benefits

  • Live Preview: See exactly what your page looks like on a mobile screen as you build it.
  • Drag-and-Drop Sorting: Rearrange your links easily to highlight your most important content.
  • Click Analytics: Logged-in users can track how many people clicked each link.
  • Custom Branding: Remove the Infinity Kit branding and use your own colors.

Why a Login is Required for Hosting

Building a temporary preview page in your browser is free. However, making that page accessible to the public via a short URL requires a hosted web server and database storage. We require a login for these features to:

  • Reserve Your Username: Claim your unique URL (e.g., infinitykit.online/u/yourname) so no one else can use it.
  • Track Your Audience: We collect anonymized data on link clicks to show you which of your social posts are driving the most traffic.
