The Shift to Paperless Verification

This tool allows you to create high-quality electronic signatures using your mouse, trackpad, or touch screen. In the modern business world, printing a document just to sign it and scan it back into the computer is a massive waste of time, paper, and ink.

Electronic signatures have become the standard for contracts, invoices, and consent forms. This tool provides a smooth, canvas-based interface to draw your signature and download it as a transparent PNG image that you can paste into any document.

🛠️

How to Use the e-Signature Tool

  1. Log In: To save your drawn signature for quick reuse or to access secure digital certificates, please log in.
  2. Use your finger or mouse to draw your signature in the box.
  3. Choose your pen color (Black, Blue, or Red) and line thickness.
  4. Click Download to save the image with a transparent background.
✨

Key Features & Benefits

  • Smooth Drawing: Vector-based canvas prevents jagged edges on your signature.
  • Transparent PNG: Downloads without a white background, making it easy to overlay on documents.
  • Saved Signatures: Logged-in users can save up to 3 different signatures for quick access.
  • Typed Signatures: Convert your typed name into a beautiful cursive font automatically.

Why a Login is Required for Saved Signatures

Drawing a signature and downloading it is a temporary operation that requires no server interaction. However, if you are signing dozens of documents a day, you don't want to draw it every time. We require a login for these features to:

  • Ensure Security: A signature is a sensitive asset. We must verify your identity before storing your signature on our servers.
  • Apply Cryptographic Certificates: Logged-in users can attach a digital certificate to their signature, proving that the document has not been altered since it was signed.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is it legally binding? ▼
Electronic signatures are legally binding in many jurisdictions under laws like the ESIGN Act and eIDAS, but you should check your local regulations.
Q: Is login required to use the tool? ▼
Yes, a free account is required to save your drawn signature for quick reuse or to access secure digital certificates for higher trust levels. Basic drawing is free.
Q: Can I use this on my phone? ▼
Yes, the canvas is fully touch-compatible, making it actually easier to use with a phone or tablet stylus.
