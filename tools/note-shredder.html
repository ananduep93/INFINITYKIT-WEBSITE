The Importance of Ephemeral Messaging

Need to share sensitive information like a password, API key, or personal message? Sending these over standard email or chat apps leaves a permanent record that can be hacked later. This tool creates a unique link that can only be opened once.

Once the recipient clicks the link and views the message, the data is instantly "shredded" from our database. It cannot be retrieved again, ensuring that your secret remains a secret. It's the digital equivalent of burning a letter after reading it.

🛠️

How to Use the Note Shredder

  1. Log In: To receive read receipts or set custom expiration timers, please log in.
  2. Type or paste your secret message into the text area.
  3. Click Create Self-Destruct Note.
  4. Copy the generated unique link.
  5. Share the link with your recipient. Warn them they can only open it once!
✨

Key Features & Benefits

  • Zero-Knowledge Storage: We cannot read your message. It is encrypted in your browser before upload.
  • One-Time Read: The link becomes invalid the exact millisecond the data is loaded.
  • Read Receipts: Logged-in users get an email notification when the link is clicked.
  • Expiration Timers: Set notes to shred after 1 hour or 24 hours even if unread.

When to Use a Note Shredder

Protect your data in these common scenarios:

🔑 Sharing Passwords

Need to give a coworker access to an account? Don't slack it. Shred it.

💳 Credit Card Details

If you must send payment details to a family member, use a self-destructing note.

🤫 Confessions

Share personal secrets or sensitive feedback without leaving a digital paper trail.

🚀

Why a Login is Required for Read Receipts

Creating a simple self-destructing link is handled by an automated database script. However, sending you an email or SMS notification when the recipient opens the link requires a background job worker and an authenticated user profile to know where to send the alert. We require a login for these features to:

  • Verify Your Identity: Ensure alerts are sent to the correct creator of the note.
  • Access Custom Expirations: Store unread notes for longer than the default 24-hour window.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How does it work? ▼
The tool creates a unique link that can only be opened once. Once viewed, the data is deleted from the database.
Q: Is login required to use the tool? ▼
Yes, a free account is required to receive read receipts or to set custom expiration timers (e.g., 24 hours). Basic shredding is free.
Q: What happens if the recipient takes a screenshot? ▼
We cannot prevent screenshots. The tool ensures the link cannot be opened again, but it cannot control the recipient's device.
