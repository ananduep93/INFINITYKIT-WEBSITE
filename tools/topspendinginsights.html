The Power of Financial Self-Awareness

This tool identifies your highest spending categories, providing critical insights into where the majority of your money is being allocated. Many people are shocked to learn that small, repeated purchases add up to more than their large monthly bills. Awareness is the first step to financial freedom.

By analyzing your transaction history, this tool isolates the "heavy hitters" in your budget. It doesn't just show you a graph; it tells you specifically which areas are draining your bank account the fastest, allowing you to make targeted cuts.

🛠️

How to Use the Insights Tool

  1. Log In: To receive personalized AI financial advice based on your top spending habits, please log in.
  2. Ensure you have logged expenses in the tracker over the last few weeks.
  3. Open this page to see the automatically generated list of top expenses.
  4. Review the suggestions on how to reduce spending in those specific areas.
✨

Key Features & Benefits

  • Automated Pareto Analysis: Identifies the 20% of categories causing 80% of your spending.
  • Trend Detection: Shows if your spending in a top category is increasing or decreasing.
  • AI Advice: Logged-in users receive custom-tailored tips to save money on their specific top categories.
  • Anonymized Benchmarking: Compare your spending percentages with other users in your income bracket.

Why a Login is Required for AI Advice

Calculating which category has the highest sum is a basic mathematical operation that runs in your browser. However, providing actionable advice requires processing your data through advanced large language models. We require a login for these features to:

  • Personalize the AI: The AI needs context about your goals (e.g., "I want to save for a house") to give useful advice about your spending.
  • Track Progress: Save the advice given so you can check back next month and see if you successfully followed the plan.
