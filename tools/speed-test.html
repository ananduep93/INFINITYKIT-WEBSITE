Understanding Speed

Internet speed is measured in Mbps (Megabits per second). Higher Mbps means faster downloads and smoother streaming. Latency (Ping) measures the delay in your connection—lower is better!

How it Works

  1. Click Start Speed Test.
  2. The tool will test your ping and simulate a download.
  3. Wait for the gauge to complete.
  4. View your final download speed and latency.
