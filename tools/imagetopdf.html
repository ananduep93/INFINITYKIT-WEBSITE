The Need for Document Standardization

This tool allows you to convert images (like JPG, PNG, and WebP) into a single, professional PDF document. Images are great for viewing, but they are terrible for sending as official documents. If you email someone 10 photos of a contract, they have to open 10 separate files.

By converting those images into a single PDF, you create a paginated, easy-to-read document that looks the exact same on every device. This is crucial for job applications, expense reports, and legal submissions.

🛠️

How to Use the Image to PDF Tool

  1. Log In: To save your conversion history or to merge more than 5 images into a single PDF, please log in.
  2. Upload or drag-and-drop the images you want to convert.
  3. Rearrange the order of the images by dragging them.
  4. Choose your page size (A4 or US Letter) and orientation.
  5. Click Generate PDF to download the finished file.
✨

Key Features & Benefits

  • No Quality Loss: Images are embedded at their native resolution without blur.
  • Drag-and-Drop Sorting: Easily change the order of pages before generating the file.
  • Bulk Conversion: Logged-in users can convert up to 50 images at once.
  • Margin Control: Add or remove white borders around your images.

Common Use Cases

Take photos of your paper receipts and combine them into a single PDF for your finance department.

Why a Login is Required for Large Batches

Converting a few images to a PDF can be done locally in your browser memory. However, processing dozens of high-resolution images can crash your browser tab due to memory limits. We require a login for these features to:

  • Use Server-Side Processing: We upload the images to our secure server to do the heavy lifting, ensuring your browser remains responsive.
  • Cloud Storage: Keep your converted PDFs saved in your account for 30 days so you can redownload them later.
