Streamlining Your Documents with PDF Merging

Combine multiple PDF files into one easily. In both professional and personal environments, dealing with multiple scattered documents can be overwhelming. Whether you are compiling a monthly report, gathering receipts for taxes, or putting together a portfolio, having everything in a single file makes sharing and reading much simpler.

Our Merge PDF tool allows you to upload several files and combine them into a single, cohesive document in seconds. You can arrange the files in the exact order you need them to appear, ensuring a smooth reading experience for your recipient.

🛠️

How to Use the PDF Merger

  1. Log In: To process files larger than 50MB or save your merge history, please log in or create a free account.
  2. Upload the PDF files you want to combine by clicking the upload area or dragging and dropping.
  3. Drag the files to arrange them in the desired order.
  4. Click the Merge button to combine the documents.
  5. Download your newly created single PDF file.
✨

Key Features & Benefits

  • Drag-and-Drop Ordering: Easily change the sequence of files before merging.
  • Large File Support: Logged-in users can process massive files up to 50MB.
  • Secure Processing: Your files are processed locally in your browser when possible, or deleted immediately after processing on our servers.
  • Fast result: Get your merged file in a split second.

Practical Use Cases

📄 Job Applications

Why a Login is Required for Large Files

Processing large PDF files, especially those with high-resolution images, requires significant server resources. We require a login for files over 50MB to:

  • Manage Server Load: Ensure the service remains fast for all users by preventing abuse.
  • Provide Cloud History: Access your merged files from any device for a limited time.
  • Advanced Features: Access tools like page rotation and deletion within the merge interface.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Can I merge password-protected PDFs? ▼
No, you must remove the password protection using a separate tool or your PDF viewer before merging the files.
Q: Is login required to use the tool? ▼
Yes, a free account is required to process files larger than 50MB or save your merge history. Basic merging for smaller files is free.
Q: Is there a limit to how many files I can merge? ▼
Free users can merge up to 5 files at a time. Logged-in users can merge up to 20 files in a single operation.
