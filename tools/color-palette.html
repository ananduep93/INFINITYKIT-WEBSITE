Why use a Palette Architect?

Great design starts with a harmonious color scheme. This tool analyzes your images and extracts the most dominant and aesthetically pleasing colors for your next project.

🛠️

How to Use

  1. Upload an image or drop it into the zone.
  2. The tool will instantly show 6 dominant hex codes.
  3. Click any color to copy its Hex code.
  4. Export the entire palette as JSON or CSS variables.
