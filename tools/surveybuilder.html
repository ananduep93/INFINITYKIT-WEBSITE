Designing Effective Surveys

This tool allows you to create custom surveys to gather feedback from your audience. Designing a good survey is an art form. You need to ask the right questions in the right way to get useful data.

With our drag-and-drop builder, you can add various question types, set validation rules, and customize the look and feel of your survey landing page. It's the easiest way to start collecting data today.

🛠️

How to Build a Survey

  1. Log In: To save your surveys, generate shareable links, and view response analytics, please log in.
  2. Enter a title and description for your survey.
  3. Click Add Question to insert a new field.
  4. Choose the question type (Text, Multiple Choice, Rating, etc.).
  5. Click Save Survey to generate your public link.
✨

Key Features & Benefits

  • Multiple Question Types: From simple text inputs to complex rating grids.
  • Logic Branching: Show or hide questions based on previous answers (Premium).
  • Custom Branding: Add your logo and brand colors to the survey page.
  • Mobile Preview: See exactly what respondents will see on their phones.
💡

Why a Login is Required for Saving

Building the structure of a survey in your browser is free. However, making that survey accessible to the public and storing the answers submitted requires a centralized database. We require a login for these features to:

🔒 Protect Your Questions

Ensure only you can edit or delete the surveys you have designed.

📊 Connect to Analytics

We need to know which account owns the survey to display the results in your private dashboard.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I add questions? ▼
Click 'Add Question' at the bottom. You can choose from text, multiple choice, or rating types.
Q: Is login required to use the tool? ▼
Yes, a free account is required to save your surveys, generate shareable links, and view response analytics. Local building is free.
Q: Can I share surveys on social media? ▼
Yes, once saved, you get a short URL that is perfect for sharing on Twitter, Facebook, or Instagram.
