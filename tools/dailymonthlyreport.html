Understanding Your Financial Rhythm

This tool allows you to view your spending habits over different time periods, providing clear daily and monthly reports for better financial tracking. Our financial lives have different cycles. Daily tracking is great for catching impulse buys, while monthly reporting is essential for evaluating fixed costs like rent and subscriptions.

By toggling between these views, you can see both the granular detail of yesterday's lunch and the high-level summary of your quarterly progress. This tool pulls data from your expense logs and structures it into beautiful, scannable reports.

🛠️

How to Use the Report Tool

  1. Log In: To view reports across all synced devices or to generate automated PDF summaries, please log in.
  2. Ensure you have entered expenses in the Expense List tool.
  3. Select the time period you want to view (Daily, Weekly, or Monthly) using the tabs.
  4. Instantly see the summarized charts and tables for that specific time.
✨

Key Features & Benefits

  • Multi-Timeframe Toggle: Switch between daily, weekly, and monthly views instantly.
  • Visual Charts: See bar graphs showing your spending trajectory over the month.
  • Automated PDFs: Logged-in users can receive a monthly summary PDF in their email.
  • Data Comparison: Compare this month's spending against last month's automatically.
💡

The Benefit of Regular Reporting

Why should you look at your financial reports regularly?

☀️ Daily Awareness

Checking your daily spend prevents the "where did my money go?" shock at the end of the month. It keeps your budget top-of-mind.

📅 Monthly Strategy

The monthly view is where you make big decisions. Are you spending too much on restaurants? Can you afford that new gym membership?

📈 Year-over-Year Growth

Compare your December spending this year to December last year to see if your financial discipline is improving.

🚀

Why a Login is Required for PDF Summaries

Generating reports in your browser using local data is free. However, running automated tasks like compiling a PDF and sending it to your email address requires server infrastructure. We require a login for these features to:

  • Identify the Recipient: We need your email address to know where to send the automated monthly PDF report.
  • Ensure Security: Financial data is sensitive. We must authenticate you before sending your report file across the web.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Can I change the start of the week? ▼
Yes, logged-in users can go to settings and choose whether their week starts on Sunday or Monday.
Q: Is login required to use the tool? ▼
Yes, a free account is required to view reports across all synced devices or to generate automated PDF summaries at the end of the month. Local reporting is free.
Q: Can I export the report to Excel? ▼
Yes, the tool allows you to download your report data as a CSV file for advanced analysis in spreadsheet software.
