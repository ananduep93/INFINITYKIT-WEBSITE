Direct & Private

Infinity Beam uses WebRTC technology to send files directly between devices. Your data never touches our servers, ensuring 100% privacy and maximum transfer speed.

🛠️

How to Use

  1. Open this page on both devices.
  2. Copy the Beam ID from the first device.
  3. Paste it into the Recipient ID box on the second device and click Connect.
  4. Once connected, select your file and watch it "beam" across!
