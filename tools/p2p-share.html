Direct & Private Peer-to-Peer Sharing

Infinity Beam uses WebRTC technology to send files directly between devices. Your data never touches our servers, ensuring 100% privacy and maximum transfer speed. Traditional file sharing often involves uploading your file to a cloud server, where it is stored and then downloaded by the recipient. This process takes double the time and introduces privacy concerns.

With P2P (Peer-to-Peer) sharing, a direct encrypted tunnel is created between the sender and the receiver. The file streams directly from one device's memory to the other's, bypassing the cloud entirely. This makes it the fastest and most secure way to move files between devices on the same desk or across the globe.

🛠️

How to Use the Infinity Beam

  1. Log In: To share files larger than 100MB or save your transfer history, please log in or create a free account.
  2. Open this page on both the sending and receiving devices.
  3. Copy the unique Beam ID generated on the receiving device.
  4. Paste it into the Recipient ID box on the sending device and click Connect.
  5. Once the status shows "Connected," drag and drop your file to send it instantly.

Key Features & Benefits

  • No Server Storage: Your files are never stored in the cloud, protecting your intellectual property.
  • End-to-End Encrypted: The WebRTC data channel is encrypted using DTLS and SRTP.
  • Cross-Platform: Works seamlessly between Android, iOS, Windows, and Mac without apps.
  • Large File Support: Logged-in users can beam massive video files and archives.

Why a Login is Required for Large Files

While the transfer itself is P2P, establishing the initial connection (signaling) requires server infrastructure (STUN/TURN servers) to help devices find each other behind firewalls. We require a login for large files to:

  • Maintain TURN Servers: Large file signaling can sometimes require relaying through our servers if a direct connection fails. This bandwidth is costly.
  • Save Transfer History: Keep a record of the filenames and sizes you have transferred for your records.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is it secure? ▼
Yes, transfers are end-to-end encrypted using the WebRTC protocol. Files stream directly from browser to browser and never touch our servers.
Q: Is login required to use the tool? ▼
Yes, a free account is required to share files larger than 100MB or save your transfer history. Basic transfers for smaller files are free.
Q: What happens if the connection breaks? ▼
Since there is no server storage, you will need to restart the transfer from the beginning if the connection is lost.
