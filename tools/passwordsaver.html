The Challenge of Password Management

This tool helps you securely save and manage your various passwords, ensuring you never lose access to your important online accounts. With the average internet user having dozens of accounts, remembering complex, unique passwords for each one is humanly impossible.

Many people resort to using simple passwords or reusing the same password across multiple sites. This creates a massive security vulnerability. Our Password Saver provides a secure digital notebook where you can keep track of your credentials without risking exposure.

🛠️

How to Use the Password Saver

  1. Log In: To save your passwords in our encrypted cloud vault for access across devices, please log in or create a free account.
  2. Enter the website name or URL and your username.
  3. Enter the corresponding password.
  4. Click the Save button to store the entry.
  5. Your entries will appear in a secure list below, masked for privacy until you choose to reveal them.
✨

Key Features & Benefits

  • Local-First Storage: By default, data is saved in your browser's local storage, meaning we can't see it.
  • Cloud Sync: Logged-in users can sync their encrypted vault across all their devices.
  • Search & Filter: Quickly find the credentials you need by searching for the website name.
  • Masked Fields: Passwords are hidden by default to prevent shoulder-surfing.
💡

Local Storage vs. Cloud Vault

Understand how your data is handled to make the best choice for your security needs:

💻 Local Storage (Default)

Data stays on your machine. It is fast and private, but if you clear your browser cache or lose your computer, the data is gone.

☁️ Cloud Vault (With Login)

Data is encrypted on your device and backed up to our servers. Access your passwords from any phone or computer securely.

🚀

Why a Login is Required for Cloud Sync

To provide cross-device access, we must store an encrypted copy of your data on our servers. We require a login for this feature to:

  • Authenticate Your Identity: Ensure that only you can pull your encrypted data down to a new device.
  • Manage Encryption Keys: Your password is part of the key used to decrypt your vault. We cannot reset it if you forget it.
❓

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is it secure? ▼
Yes, your data is saved locally on your own device by default. Cloud-synced data is end-to-end encrypted.
Q: Is login required to use the tool? ▼
Yes, a free account is required to save your passwords in our encrypted cloud vault for access across devices. Local saving is free.
Q: Can you see my passwords? ▼
No. Even if you use cloud sync, the data is encrypted on your device before being sent to us. We hold no keys to read your data.
