Unlocking Hidden Data in Your Photos

This tool provides detailed information about your images, including file size, format, resolution, and hidden metadata (Exif data), all in one clear view. Every time you take a photo with a smartphone or digital camera, the device embeds a wealth of information inside the file. This data, known as Exif (Exchangeable Image File Format), includes technical details about the shot and sometimes even location coordinates.

Our Image Info tool acts as a detective for your files. Simply upload an image, and we will extract and display all the technical specifications and metadata stored within it, helping you understand the file better and protect your privacy.

🛠️

How to Use the Image Info Tool

  1. Log In: To save your analysis history or process large files above 10MB, please log in or create a free account.
  2. Upload the image you want to examine from your device by clicking the upload area or dragging and dropping the file.
  3. View the comprehensive information displayed about your file, organized by categories (General, Camera, Location).
  4. Toggle privacy settings if you want to strip metadata before downloading (feature available for logged-in users).
✨

Key Features & Benefits

  • Exif Data Extraction: See camera model, aperture, shutter speed, and ISO settings used for the shot.
  • Location Mapping: If the photo contains GPS tags, view where the photo was taken on a map.
  • Format Identification: Confirm if the file is a true JPG, PNG, or if the extension has been changed.
  • Privacy Protection: Learn what data you are sharing before posting photos online.

Practical Use Cases

Why should you check your image info? Here are some common scenarios:

🔒 Privacy Checks

Check if photos taken at home contain your GPS coordinates before listing items for sale on public marketplaces.

📸 Photography Learning

Study the camera settings of your best shots to understand how aperture and shutter speed affect the final image.

🌐 Web Optimization

Check the exact resolution and file size to ensure images are optimized for fast loading on websites.

Why a Login is Required for Large Files

Reading complex Exif data from high-resolution RAW files or large images requires significant processing power and memory. We require a login for files over 10MB to:

  • Handle Heavy Files: Process large files on our dedicated servers rather than crashing your mobile browser.
  • Save History: Keep a log of images you have analyzed for comparison later.
  • Advanced Stripping: Access tools to remove specific metadata fields selectively before sharing.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Does it change the image? ▼
No, this tool only reads the metadata of the image and does not modify the file in any way during the inspection phase.
Q: Is it fast? ▼
Yes, for standard files, the information is processed instantly within your browser using local JavaScript.
Q: Is login required to use the tool? ▼
Yes, a free account is required to save your analysis history or process files larger than 10MB. Basic info for small files is free.
