The Rise of QR Codes

A dynamic QR code allows you to share more than just text. You can share Wi-Fi credentials, WhatsApp start-chats, and even full Contact Cards (vCards) with a single scan. QR (Quick Response) codes have transitioned from a niche marketing tool to an essential part of daily life, bridging the physical and digital worlds.

From restaurant menus to contactless payments and event check-ins, QR codes offer a frictionless way to transmit data. Our generator allows you to create high-quality QR codes for various use cases instantly.

Static vs. Dynamic QR Codes

Understanding the difference is crucial for your project:

🔒 Static QR Codes

The data is hardcoded directly into the square pattern. Once printed, it cannot be changed. Best for permanent links like website homepages.

🔄 Dynamic QR Codes

The code contains a short URL that redirects to your target. You can change the destination URL anytime without reprinting the code. Requires login.

🚀

Why a Login is Required for Dynamic Codes

Dynamic QR codes require a redirection server to handle the traffic and allow link modifications. This infrastructure costs money to maintain. We require a login for this feature to:

  • Provide a Dashboard: So you can manage your active codes and update their destination links.
  • Track Scan Analytics: See how many times your code was scanned and from which locations.
